EcoPulse 2025

EcoPulse 2025 is more than just an event—it's a movement. Held at the heart of Panjim, this year’s edition brings together changemakers, innovators, and everyday citizens to explore bold solutions for a sustainable future.

From interactive workshops and green tech showcases to community clean-up drives and climate talks, EcoPulse 2025 is designed to spark awareness and inspire action. Whether you're an environmental enthusiast, a curious learner, or an organization looking to make a difference, this is your space to connect, collaborate, and contribute.

With a streamlined booking experience and inclusive programming, we’ve made it easier than ever to be part of the change. Join us as we pulse with purpose—and turn ideas into impact.
